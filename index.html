Home

I am currently a PhD student at IMPA, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. I am under the supervision of Henrique Bursztyn. I have also spent some time (Aug-Dec/2015) at UIUC under the supervision of Rui Loja Fernandes.

I'm interested in symplectic/poisson geometry, Lie algebroids and groupoids, Courant algebroids, deformation cohomology, normal forms and splitting theorems.

Papers and preprints:

  • Splitting theorems for Poisson and related structures (with H. Bursztyn and E. Meinrenken). Preprint.
